About

I am an incoming Ph.D. student in Artificial Intelligence at the Southwestern University of Finance and Economics (enrolling Fall 2026). I received my M.Sc. in Artificial Intelligence and Adaptive Systems with Distinction from the University of Sussex, where I was awarded the Dean's Honour Medal of the Artificial Intelligence Institute.

My research centers on building machine learning systems that are trustworthy and reliable under uncertainty, including two solo-authored papers at ICML 2026 and UAI 2026. I am broadly interested in conformal prediction, generative-model priors, and world models.

Research Interests

  • Trustworthy generative AI: retrieval-augmented generation, citation faithfulness, and risk control
  • Conformal prediction and uncertainty quantification under distribution shift
  • Reliable machine learning with generative priors: diffusion-model priors and calibration-aware learning
  • World models and reinforcement learning
